About:

Ryan J. Squal is a 21 year old electronic producer/songwriter originally from the Chicago area, now based in Pittsburgh. Influenced by jazz, soul, and hip-hop, he blends these sounds to create his own uniqe style. He has been playing music since a very young age, around four or five years old, and took interest in learning multiple instruments such as piano, guitar, trumpet, bass, etc.

It wasn't until 2016 that he began recording music and making beats, using an MPC given to him by his older brother. Initially, he only made sample heavy boom bap beats, with great inspiration from the works of legendary producers such as 9th Wonder and J Dilla. Eventually learning to record and incorporate instruments, his style evolved into a blend of different genres. He is always looking to push his sound further and expand his influences.
